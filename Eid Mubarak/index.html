About Eid

Eid is a special occasion celebrated by Muslims all around the world. It marks the end of Ramadan, a month of fasting, prayer, and reflection. Join us in celebrating this joyous occasion and learn more about its significance.

Significance of Eid

Eid Al Fitr, also known as the "Festival of Breaking the Fast," is a time for family, friends, and communities to come together in a spirit of gratitude and joy. It is a time to give thanks for the strength to complete the month of fasting and to seek forgiveness and peace.
